# 👽 NetherSX2-TurnipEnchanted 🔮

Welcome to the **TurnipEnchanted** repository. This is a specialized, standalone **NetherSX2 Turnip** fork focused on driver experimentation, compatibility tuning, and stability for modern Adreno GPUs.

---

### ❓ The simple Idea

I'm just a small-scale modder. My goal here is **not** to replace anyone’s work, nor am I asking for donations or anything of the sort. 

This project was born out of a personal need, and I figured it could help others—but above all, I hope it can help the developers who are carrying this emulator forward with such dedication. Massive respect to them for the work they put in.

### 🔧 What do I actually do?
I update the **Turnip drivers**. That’s it. It’s a simple process, but it can radically change how the emulator communicates with your hardware.

Think of this app as a **field test**. If my build proves stable, I hope to help the original devs integrate these improvements. They will surely do a cleaner, more optimized job than I ever could, and hopefully, great things will come from this tiny project.

### 🎯 My Stance
I’m here to help the community. I hope this project is seen for what it is—a way to contribute and test new ground. I don't want to step on anyone's toes or start drama. If I ever feel like I'm doing more harm than good, I’ll take a step back, no questions asked. 

**Let’s just keep things simple, transparent, and keep moving forward.**
